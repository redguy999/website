Basic Dungeon Crawler
X

Controls:

Use the arrow keys to move, your character is the "X" on the grid.
Use spacebar to interact with the exit, or pick up an item.
Click on equipment in your inventory to equip it.
Click on a consumable to use it on yourself.
Click and drag a consumable to an enemy to use it on the enemy.

Inventory:

If an item is scrolled/hovered and it changes color, that means it does something interesting. Blue colored items are equipment. You may equip equipment by either dragging it to the inventory, or by clicking it.
Orange colored items are consumables. Clicking on a consumable will cause it to be used on yourself.
Light green items are both a type of equipment and a consumable, clicking on one will cause it to be equiped, you'll have to drag it to the tile you are on in order to use it on yourself.

Tile types:

The starting tile is Light green, theres nothing special about them beyond being the start tiles
An exit tiles is red, interacting (spacebar) while standing on one will bring you to the next floor. You can not return to a floor you have already visited!
Treasure tiles are gold colored, interact while standing on the same tile as the treasure to pick the item they have.
Black tiles are walls, you can't walk through them.
White tiles are empty.
A tile of any other color is an enemy.

Goal:

Get better and better gear as you go deeper in this mysterious Dungeon
